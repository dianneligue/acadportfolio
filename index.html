Biographical Sketch

Kim Dianne Ligue

Assistant Professor of Applied Mathematics
University of the Philippines Mindanao

She graduated magna cum laude with a Bachelor of Science degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of the Philippines Mindanao in 2016. Under the CHED-CMUA full scholarship, she pursued her Master of Science degree in Information Technology at Carnegie Mellon University Australia in 2019 where she graduated with the Highest Distinction Award. 

Her research interests are Deep Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Spatiotemporal Data Analysis, Parametric and Non-parametric Statistics, and Operations Research. She is also currently the adviser of the UP Mindanao Society of Math Majors.
